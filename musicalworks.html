Los Sadicos

It was inspiring to play with such talented musicians. I originally joined the band after their former lead guitarist had left and Edgar, the lead singer, proposed that I'd fill in for an upcoming performance. I had to learn their whole set a day before the show which was quite a nerve wrecking propisition. Thankfully I had already been playing guitar for some time and I was able to get down all the songs.

Los Sadicos' rendition of "Cementario"

Joining the band

As more gigs popped up for the band, I continued filling and had the opportunity to become one of the members. After playing with the band for a while, I realized how impactful every instrument is to make the song come together. Each instrument is just as important as the other and it wouldn't sound the same otherwise. Gaining this perspective has allowed me to develop a greater appreciation and passion for music.
