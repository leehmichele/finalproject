Getaway Cabin

I recently took an interest in tiny house living and wanted to experience what it would be like. Alex and I stayed at this cabin in Shenandoah to get away from the fast paced lifestyle and bustling crowds of the suburbs. We enjoyed waking up to the nature scenery and hiking in the trails nearby. Our visit there was peaceful as we had thoughtful conversations accompanied by the sounds of nature.

Here's a polaroid I took of Alex. He collabs with many people and frequently partakes in the DC photography meets.
